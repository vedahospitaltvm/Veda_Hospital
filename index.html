OUR TREATMENTS


ABHYANGAM

Full body massage Anointing the body with oil.Promotion and regulation of proper functioning of vata.Helps in nourishment of the body and promotes longevity.Improves the function of vital organs and tissues.Siroabhyanga(Head massage) and Pada abhyanga (foot and sole massage) are included.

VARIOUS SWEDA KARMAS (KIZHI)

Sudation process.Various drugs are made into bolus heated in oil and applied to the body in lukewarm stage.Njavara Kizhi, Naranga kizhi, Ela kizhi, Mamsa kizhi etc are used Accor to various conditions.

SHIRODHARA

Medicated oil ,milk, buttermilk or kashaya poured in continous stream of drip on the head in a specific manner. Relieves stress,psychosomatic disorders,neurological disorders,psychiatric disorders.

GREEVA VASTI

Retaining the oil in the nape of neck.Indicated in cervical spondylosis,neck stiffness

KATI VASTI

Oil retained in the lowback region. Relieves lowback pain, IVDP cases, lumbar spondylosis

Info

Ayurveda exist even before the vedic period. The aim of Ayurveda is the maintenance of health in a healthy man and the relief from disease in a patient. The healthy state of man has been clearly defined in Ayurveda. A person whose somatic and psychic humours are in the state of equilibrium, digestive capacity or digestion is uniformly healthy, with normal functions of the fundamental tissues of the body and waste products accompanied by the lucid status of the soul,cognitive organs and mind is said to be a healthy person.
