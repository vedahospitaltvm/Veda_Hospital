OUR TREATMENTS



ABHYANGAM

Full body massage Anointing the body with oil.Promotion and regulation of proper functioning of vata.Helps in nourishment of the body and promotes longevity.Improves the function of vital organs and tissues.Siroabhyanga(Head massage) and Pada abhyanga (foot and sole massage) are included.

VARIOUS SWEDA KARMAS (KIZHI)

Sudation process.Various drugs are made into bolus heated in oil and applied to the body in lukewarm stage.Njavara Kizhi, Naranga kizhi, Ela kizhi, Mamsa kizhi etc are used Accor to various conditions.

SHIRODHARA

Medicated oil ,milk, buttermilk or kashaya poured in continous stream of drip on the head in a specific manner. Relieves stress,psychosomatic disorders,neurological disorders,psychiatric disorders.

SHIROVASTHI

To retain or hold medicated oil on the scale for specific time. Indicated in insomnia,facial paralysis, numbness of head,headache.

DHANYAMLA DHARA

Lukewarm dhanyamla is poured over the whole body.Indicated in joint disorders, inflammatory conditions.

KSHEERA DHARA

Medicated milk is poured over the whole body.Indicated in insanity,sleeplessness.

GREEVA VASTI

Retaining the oil in the nape of neck.Indicated in cervical spondylosis,neck stiffness

KATI VASTI

Oil retained in the lowback region. Relieves lowback pain, IVDP cases, lumbar spondylosis

KASHAYA VASTI

Medicated kashaya is given through rectal route as enema. Mainly done for Shodana purpose(cleansing therapy),rhematologic disorders.

NASYA KARMA

Medicated oil, kwatha,swarasa,churna are administered through nostril.Cures stiffness of head, cervical complaints,headache etc.

SNEHAPANA

Medicated ghee is given to the patient according to the disease in a specific dose by increasing day by day. Improves appetite, digestion, keeps body light and brings softness to the body.

THALAM

Application of medicated oil mixed with certain herbal drugs on the scalp for certain period of time. Cures psychiatric disorders, insomnia psychosomatic disorders,cervical spondylosis headache.

UDWARTHANAM

Reinforced rubbing of the body by churna. Indicated in obesity, neurological conditions.

YONI PRAKSHALANAM

Douching the vagina with medicated kashaya according to various conditions. Indicated in vaginal infections and infertility.

YONI PICHU

Medicated oil or kashaya dipped in cotton and keep in the vagina for certain time periods Indicated in various vaginal infections and infertility.

YONI DHOOPANAM

Medicated drugs are fired and the smoke is administered to the vagina for specific time.Indicated in infections in vagina and infertility according to various symptoms of the patients

YONI POORANAM

Medicated drugs are made into paste and filled into the vagina for certain time. Relieves infections in vagina and good for infertility.

BANDAGING

Done for immobilisation of joints.Especially done in fractures and dislocations.


Info

Ayurveda exist even before the vedic period. The aim of Ayurveda is the maintenance of health in a healthy man and the relief from disease in a patient. The healthy state of man has been clearly defined in Ayurveda. A person whose somatic and psychic humours are in the state of equilibrium, digestive capacity or digestion is uniformly healthy, with normal functions of the fundamental tissues of the body and waste products accompanied by the lucid status of the soul,cognitive organs and mind is said to be a healthy person.
